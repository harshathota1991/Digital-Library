DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF THE TEST

Concept Tested On Description Result
Background and layout A check was made to identify the user friendliness of the background color and its acceptability. Also, a synchronization between the background color and images on the website was checked. Background color and images and layout are acceptable
Text Readability of the textual information on each webpage was identified by questioning the testers. Appropriate spacing between consecutive lines and was considered while developing the website prototype. Text layout is acceptable.
Text Color The contrast of background color and the text color was identified by questioning the testers. Text colors are acceptable.
Text Font Readability of the textual information on a webpage was identified by questioning the testers. Appropriate spacing between consecutive lines and text fonts was also considered while developing the website prototype. Text Font is acceptable.
Links The links were checked to ensure that no link points to blank or broken page. Also, since some of the links had a blank target and opened in separate browser or a browser tab, a check was made to ensure that previous page was not lost. Based upon the background color, the testers were asked whether they could easily identify links on web page. Links are clearly visible and there are no blank and broken pages
Images As there are many images in the website, the alignment of the images was tested by questioning the testers. All images are acceptable. An increase of the image width and height in the home page might be good.
Browser Compatibility The website was tested on different browsers such as Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome and Safari The website is consistent on different browsers and browser compatibility is good.
