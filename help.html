Help/ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



What are absolute classes?

The users have looked for live online training to improve their skills at a steeper learning curve. Absolute classes is an effort to bring quality education online on technical and non-technical subjects and deliver remarkable learning experience. At Absolute Classes, we strive hard to ensure the learner moves up the skill level at an accelerated pace.

Why absolute classes?

Absolute classes has been delivering simple and easy learning material. Our training modules have been designed to provide maximum learning experience in minimum effort and time. By combining live online training – Absolute Classes with Tutorials Library and Coding Ground ( Online Terminal and IDE), we ensure that the learner moves up the skill level at an accelerated pace.

What if I did not like the absolute classes?

We are confident that you will love the way we teach online and also you will find our classes are worth your time and effort. But if you are unsatisfied with a course, you can cancel your subscription after attending the first session. to unsubscribe, Just drop an email to contact@digitallibrary.com

What are the system requirements for attending the absolute classes?

On a PC:

  • Windows 8, 7, Vista, XP.
  • A microphone and speakers. (A USB headset is recommended.)
  • Dual-core 2.4GHz CPU or faster with 4GB of RAM
  • Internet connection with atleast 1Mbps of speed.
  • Internet Explorer 7.0 or newer, Mozilla Firefox 4.0 or newer or Google Chrome 5.0 or newer

On a MAC:

  • Mac OS X 10.6 – Snow Leopard or newer.
  • Intel processor (1GB of RAM or better recommended)
  • Internet connection with atleast 1Mbps of speed.
  • A microphone and speakers. (A USB headset is recommended.)
  • Safari 3.0 or newer, Firefox 4.0 or newer or Google Chrome 5.0 or newer

Certification for the course attended ?

Yes! you will be awarded with a course completion certificate as long as you attended all the allotted classes. Once you complete a course, certificate will be accessible from your account in the form of JPG, which can be downloaded or printed for future references.

What if any sessions are missed?

You can always attend your class or change your class to any other time slot based on your convenience. You can stop attending the course in between, drop us an email at contact@digitallibrary.com and when the next batch gets scheduled, we will reserve a slot for you.
